Odin Recipes

Ingredients

    Chili Seasoning
  • 1/2 tablespoons of Ground Cumin
  • 1/2 tablespoons of Dried Oregano
  • 1 tablespoons of Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp of Cajun Seasoning or to taste (i.e. Slap Ya Mama)
    Chili
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped yellow onion
  • 1 cup of chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 7-oz can of chopped green chiles or 1/4 cup of jalapeno peppers; adjust amount if you like spice
  • 2 lbs of ground beef
  • 3 cloves of fresh garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of granulated Sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp of hot sauce, optional
  • 1 28-oz can of chopped whole tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 14.5-oz can of diced Tomatoes, undrained
  • 2 cups of Beef Stock or Beef Broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3 15-oz cans of light kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • Kosher salt and cracked black pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Mix together chili seasonings, set aside.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large heavy bottomed soup pot over medium high heat. Add the onion, bell pepper, and green chiles or jalapenos; saute until tender, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook for about 10 minutes or until meat is no longer pink. Drain off excess fat.
  4. Add the garlic and cook another minute.
  5. Add the chili seasonings, sugar, Worcestershire, hot sauce, tomatoes, beef stock and bay leaves; bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for about 40 minutes or until liquid is significantly reduced.
  7. Drain one can of the kidney beans, mash and add to the chili. Drain and rinse the remaining two cans of beans and add to the chili unmashed.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste, adjust and add previous seasons as needed.
  9. Continue cooking on a low simmer another 20 minutes.
  10. Serve with toppings of choice. Cheese, sour cream and green onion make the perfect toppings.
