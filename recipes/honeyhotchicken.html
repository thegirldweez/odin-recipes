Odin Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2lbs chicken wings
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup hot honey
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Directions

  1. In a bowl, combine the flour, 2 teaspoons salt, the onion powder, garlic powder and cayenne pepper. Add the chicken wings and toss to coat.
  2. Heat 2 inches of vegetable oil in a pot until it registers 350Ëš F on a deep-fry thermometer. Working in batches, add the wings and fry until browned, 8 to 12 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on a rack set on a baking sheet.
  3. Whisk the melted butter, hot honey, vinegar and red pepper flakes in a large bowl. Add the wings and toss to coat.
  4. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
